Process

From enquiry to income in four straightforward steps

We've built our process to be fast, clear, and completely hassle-free for landlords. Most agreements are agreed within a week of first contact.

1

Get in touch

Contact us by phone, WhatsApp, or our enquiry form. There's no obligation and no pressure. We'll have a quick conversation about your property and goals, and answer any questions you have about how the model works.

📞 Call us 💬 WhatsApp
2

Property visit and offer

We visit your property at a time that suits you. We assess the condition, location, and type of property, and discuss any light preparation that might be needed. We then provide a written guaranteed rent offer — usually within 24–48 hours.

Our offer is transparent. We explain how we arrived at the figure and what the agreement includes. There are no hidden charges and no management fees deducted from your rent.

3

Agreement signed

If you're happy with our offer, we proceed to agreement. We use a professionally drafted lease agreement that protects both parties clearly. We encourage you to have it reviewed by your own solicitor if you wish — transparency is important to us.

The agreement covers the rent amount, payment dates, duration (typically 3–5 years), responsibilities, break clauses, and what happens at the end of the agreement.

4

Your rent arrives — every month

From your agreed start date, your guaranteed rent is paid on the same date each month. We handle everything else. You don't need to call us, check in, or manage anything. Your only job is to check your bank account.

We provide clear communication throughout the agreement and are always available if you have questions. But our goal is to give you a genuinely hands-off experience.

Our story

Why we started Dobson Homes

Dobson Homes was created because too many landlords are stuck in the same exhausting cycle — chasing rent, fielding calls at all hours, navigating disputes, and spending money they hadn't budgeted for.

The traditional letting model wasn't designed for landlords who want income, not a second job. And for retired landlords or those with growing portfolios, the time and energy demanded by tenant management becomes unsustainable.

We created Dobson Homes to offer something genuinely different: a long-term agreement where we take on the property, pay the rent, and let you get on with your life.

We're building this business on transparency and professional standards — not on overpromising or inflated rent figures. When we make you an offer, it's one we can sustain. When we sign an agreement, we honour it.

Dobson Homes is a new business, and we're proud of that. It means we're building our reputation from the ground up — earning every landlord's trust through clear communication, reliable payments, and professional property care.
